## 1. The Elevator Pitch
**Tagline:** Your College Application Command Center.
**Problem:** The college application process is fragmented across spreadsheets, dozens of browser tabs, and disconnected checklists, leading to massive student stress and missed deadlines.
**Solution:** Waypoint centralizes the entire journey into a single, high-end dashboard. It combines AI-powered strategy, essay narration, and a unified command center to give students total control over their future.

---

## 2. Key Features (The "Wow" Factors)
*   **AI Command Center:** Integrated AI counselor that helps with discovery, tactical strategy, and conceptual essay review.
*   **Interactive Waypoint Timeline:** A beautiful, cursor-reactive path that visualizes the student's journey from discovery to acceptance.
*   **Unified Essay Workspace:** A distraction-free environment for drafting essays with real-time feedback.
*   **Smart Calendar & Deadlines:** Automated tracking of application milestones and essay deadlines.
*   **Premium Aesthetics:** A state-of-the-art "floating" design system with glassmorphism and full system-theme synchronization.

---

## 3. Technical Stack (For the Judges)
*   **Frontend:** Vanilla JS, HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Design System).
*   **Backend:** Node.js API (Railway hosted).
*   **Database/Auth:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) for real-time data and secure authentication.
*   **Payments:** Stripe integration for premium feature tiers.
*   **AI Engine:** Integrated AI models for conceptual essay review and application strategy.
*   **Deployment:** Vercel (Frontend) & Railway (Backend).

---

## 4. Demo Recording Script (3-Minute Roadmap)
1.  **The Hook (0:00-0:30):** Start on the Landing Page. Follow the **Interactive Timeline** with your cursor. Show how the line paints itself as you scroll.
2.  **The Core (0:30-1:30):** Log in to the Dashboard. Highlight the "Quick Actions" and the current application status. Show the **Smart Calendar** with upcoming deadlines.
3.  **The Secret Sauce (1:30-2:30):** Open the **Essay Workspace**. Type a few lines and mention how the AI provides conceptual feedback (not just grammar checking). 
4.  **The Polish (2:30-3:00):** Quickly toggle **Dark/Light Mode** to show off the seamless theme sync. End on the "Ready to Take Control" CTA.

---

## 5. Potential "Judge Questions" & Answers
*   **How does this differ from Common App?** "Common App is the registration tool; Waypoint is the preparation and strategy tool. We bridge the gap between 'I want to apply' and 'I am ready to submit'."
*   **What was the hardest technical challenge?** "Building the interactive timeline that accurately tracks path length versus cursor Y-position for a 1:1 fluid feeling."
